GFRIEND(여자친구)

is a South Korean Girl Group formed by Source Music in 2015, the group consisted of six members: Sowon(leader & main rapper), Yerin(lead dancer & sub vocal), Eunha(lead vocal), Yuju(main vocal & lead dancer), SinB(main dancer) and Umji(sub vocal & lead rapper).

The debuted with the EP "Season of Glass" with the title track being Glass Bead and won several rookie awards, garnering momentum early on despite being from a small company.

The group disbanded on May 22, 2021 after all six members of the group left the copmany upon the end of their contracts.
